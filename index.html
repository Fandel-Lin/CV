Fandel Lin

M.S. in CCE from NCKU, Taiwan

Fandel Lin receives the M.S. degree in Institute of Computer and Communication Engineering (CCE) at National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), Taiwan. His research interests lie in Urban Computing, Data Mining, Geographic Information Science, and Combinatorial Optimization. Meanwhile, his researches are recognized by the first place award of Student Research Competition in ACM SIGSPATIAL'18 and the second place award of ACM Student Research Competition Grand Final in 2018-19.
